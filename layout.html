OpenCVGenerator is a solution for all those people who need to make or update their curriculums, but simply cannot find a free option that saves them the time and effort of organizing and writing their CV's.

How to use it?

Why use OpenCVGenerator

No account needed

There is no need to sign up in order to be able to generate your curriculum. Just go straight into working on your curriculum.

Secure

Because there is no need for an account your data won't be stored anywhere other than your own browser and will be deleted once you export your curriculum

Really free

There are no hidden cost. You will not be asked for a payment in order to download your curriculum. You simply generate your curriculum and export it.

Future plans

Self-hosting

Because there are not registration to use the service it becomes even easier to be able to deploy the app on your own device. Coming soon...

Importing curriculum

The whole idea of OpenCVGenerator is that you can create or update your curriculum as fast and simple as possible, therefore the ability to simply import your data to get to editing as fast as possible is something to look for. Coming soon...

How to use it
Learn how to use OpenCVGenerator in 4 simple steps
  1. Select one of the available curriculum templates.
  2. Fill the form with your academic and professional achievements.
  3. Customize your curriculum with different fonts or colors.
  4. Export as a pdf ready to send.
